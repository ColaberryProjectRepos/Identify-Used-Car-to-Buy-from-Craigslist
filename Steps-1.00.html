Problem Statement 1

Craigslist is the world's largest online collection of used cars and trucks for sale, and one major challenge is that it is very difficult to collect all of them in the same place. So there is need to build and maintain this dataset over the years in order to keep track of the unique behavior of used vehicle sub-industry of the larger automotive industry.  The current practice is that this data is scraped every few months. Craigslist boasts over 52 million unique, ready-to-buy shoppers each month—more than Cars.com and AutoTrader combined. To guide their decision making given their budget they are looking at a whole raft of preferences including condition, vehicle type, color, year of manufacture and so on.  With the average U.S. car shopper now spending 11 hours online and only 3.5 hours offline, it’s clear shoppers are trying to do everything they can until they actually have to visit your lot and buy the car.
